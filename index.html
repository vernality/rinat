About Me

I started programming and made my first website when I was 12. Following in my father's footsteps, I entered the National Research Nuclear University MEPhl and received a first-class fundamental education. I became more interested in programming and I solved a couple of research puzzles, such as calculation of the Uranium-Plutonium phase diagram. I started as a software engineer and was employed in one of the top Russian Internet companies while studying at the university.

I started iOS development in 2011 during iOS 4: the brave old days of manual memory management. My first app was a complex 3D visualisation project of phase diagrams for the Russian Academy of Sciences. I have finished more than 10 courses from top US universities on Coursera and edX and earned my first money in the App Store (my apps are still bringing me some profit). And I started freelancer career in 2015. In 2020 I'm Upwork Top Rated Mobile Premium Cloud Freelancer.

Nuclear physics is an interest of mine, but my love of programming is stronger. My values don't let me found body-shop in the field of software development, that's why I founded Vernality - open source Startup Studio. The question - how to divide profit based on participant efforts is the main question for me now. While still working as a developer, I'm trying to make the world better. Join our community!
